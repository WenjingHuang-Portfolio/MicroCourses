Introduction:

The Cyber Security Fundamentals course provides a thorough introduction to the essential practices and technologies used to safeguard digital assets. Students will gain an understanding of network security protocols, which are designed to protect data during transmission. The course covers key concepts such as threat analysis, which involves assessing vulnerabilities and identifying potential risks, and risk management practices that help organizations mitigate and respond to cyber incidents. Additionally, learners will study common security tools and ethical considerations in cybersecurity practices. This 30-hour course, available over 2 months of self-paced learning, is perfect for beginners and IT professionals aiming to enhance their understanding of how to defend against cyber threats. The course lays the groundwork for further advanced cybersecurity training and certifications.

Course Content:

  • Introduction to CyberSecurity
  • Types of Cyber Threats
  • Security Technologies
  • Risk Assessment Strategies
  • Case Studies
